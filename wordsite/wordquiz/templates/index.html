Welcome to Word Quiz!

Isn't it boring to study words? With Word Quiz, it's still boring! However, it's easier, faster to learn and you can do it anywhere where you have your smartphone or computer.

Create new Quiz

How does it work?

It's really simple: A word of your desired set of words is shown in one language, and you're supposed to remember it in the other language. After you try to remember the word, you can reveal the word and click "CORRECT" if you got it correct and otherwise "INCORRECT".

On correct guesses, the word won't be asked anymore. When the guess was incorrect, the word will be asked after a while, but not so late that it's impossible to remember.
